FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about nail services at Beauty Hive Studio

Q: What services do you offer?

We specialize in Gel-X extensions, acrylic nails (full sets and fills), builder gel manicures, custom hand-painted nail art, and luxury pedicures. All services are performed in our private, appointment-only studio in the College Area of San Diego.

Q: What is the policy on fills and repairs?

Fills are recommended every 2-3 weeks to maintain the health of your nails. Please note: If you come in with nails older than 3 weeks, I won't be able to do fills as they would require a new set instead. Repairs for broken nails are complimentary for the first 3 days after your appointment.

Q: Do I need to remove my old set before getting new nails?

A removal fee will be added if a removal service is required. This ensures proper prep and application for your new Gel-X, acrylic, or builder gel set.

Q: How do I care for my custom nail art?

To maintain your custom nail art, apply cuticle oil daily and a clear top coat every few days. Avoid picking or filing your nails, as this can damage the design and the nail bed. Your nail artist will provide detailed aftercare instructions.
