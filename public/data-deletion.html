Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

Important: We collect minimal data

The Beauty Hive Instagram integration only displays our own business account's public Instagram posts. We do not collect, store, or process personal data from website visitors or Instagram users.

What Data We Collect

Our Instagram integration only accesses:

  • Our own Instagram posts - Public images, captions, and engagement metrics from @isssbeenails
  • Temporary browser cache - Posts cached in your browser's local storage for 1 hour

We do NOT collect:

  • Visitor Instagram login information
  • Personal user data from Instagram
  • Instagram profiles other than our own
  • Any data that requires user authentication

Data Deletion Options

Option 1: Clear Browser Data (Instant)

Since we only store data in your browser's local storage, you can delete it instantly:

For Chrome, Edge, Brave:

  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  2. Select "All time" or "Last hour"
  3. Check "Cookies and other site data"
  4. Click "Clear data"

For Firefox:

  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  2. Select "Everything"
  3. Check "Cookies" and "Site Data"
  4. Click "Clear Now"

For Safari:

  1. Go to Safari ‚Üí Preferences ‚Üí Privacy
  2. Click "Manage Website Data"
  3. Search for our website
  4. Click "Remove" or "Remove All"

Option 2: Automatic Deletion

Cached Instagram post data is automatically deleted after 1 hour. Simply close your browser and the data will expire.

Facebook/Instagram App Data Deletion

If you used our Facebook app for Instagram integration and want to revoke access:

To remove app access from your Instagram account:

  1. Go to Instagram Settings ‚Üí Security ‚Üí Apps and Websites
  2. Find "The Beauty Hive" or our app name
  3. Click "Remove"

To remove app access from Facebook:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings ‚Üí Apps and Websites
  2. Find our app
  3. Click "Remove"

Note: This only applies if you are the business owner who connected our app to Instagram. Regular website visitors do not need to do this.
